About Us

At Dr Rossinski Dental Health, we believe great dentistry begins with trust, expertise, and a deep commitment to patient well-being. As a leading Dentist in West Village and a respected Holistic Dentist NYC, our practice is built around a philosophy that blends advanced clinical excellence with a compassionate, whole-body approach to oral health. For years, we have proudly served individuals and families throughout Manhattan, helping them achieve healthier smiles while supporting overall wellness.


Our Philosophy: Dentistry That Supports Total Health

Oral health is a cornerstone of whole-body health, and our practice is designed around that belief. Instead of focusing solely on symptoms, we look for root causes, patterns, and lifestyle factors that influence dental conditions. This holistic, integrative mindset allows us to create personalized treatment plans that support long-term function and wellness.

As a Holistic Dentist NYC, Dr. Rossinski emphasizes non-toxic materials, minimally invasive techniques, and considerate treatment planning that respects both the mouth and the rest of the body. Whether we are restoring a damaged tooth, transforming a smile, or guiding patients through preventive care, our goal is always to protect your health as a complete system—not just your teeth.

Our Commitment to Safety and Biocompatibility

Holistic dentistry requires a careful and informed approach to materials and techniques. Our practice carefully selects products that are biocompatible, metal-free, and designed to reduce inflammation and toxicity. This is especially valuable for individuals with sensitivities, immune concerns, or a preference for natural health practices.

We also incorporate treatment philosophies that minimize unnecessary drilling, conserve healthy tooth structure, and support natural healing whenever possible.
